## Summary

The qualifier has two deliverables:

1. **IEEE Conference Paper** (5-6 pages) -- Literature survey of 6 papers with research proposal
2. **Oral Presentation** (15 min + 5 min Q&A) -- High-level coverage of paper content

See `qualifier-requirements.md` for:

- 50+ functional and non-functional requirements with tracking IDs
- 29 acceptance criteria across both deliverables
- 12 identified risks with mitigations
- Dependency graph and task ordering constraints
- Verification checkpoints for each project phase
- Traceability matrix mapping requirements to phases

## Document Naming Convention

Use descriptive names:

- `{topic}-checklist.md` - Verification checklists
- `{area}-requirements.md` - Area-specific standards
- `{quality}-criteria.md` - Quality acceptance criteria
